Security & Privacy

Your trust is the product.

Every architectural decision prioritizes the security of your Gmail data. Sensitive information never persists beyond your active session.

Start for free

Encrypted session cookies

Authentication payloads are encrypted with Fernet symmetric encryption before being stored in your browser cookie.

Read-only Gmail scope

The OAuth scope only permits reading messages. We cannot send, delete, modify, or access drafts.

Safe UI rendering

Dashboard cards are built from normalized, sanitized data. Raw HTML from email content is never injected.

No persistent storage

Email bodies are processed in memory only. When your session ends, all data is gone.
